# wrapper-chain

Demonstrates a high reader-friction flow.

Path: `cli.main → handlers.handle → services.run → repos.fetch → db.query`.

- Three middle functions are **thin wrappers** (pass through without logic).
- Five files crossed.
- Expected: high `trace_depth`, high `file_jumps`, `wrapper_depth ≥ 3`.

The `expected.json` and `expected.md` goldens are generated by running
`rf scan spec/examples/wrapper-chain/project` with
`UPDATE_GOLDEN=1` after the implementation is stable. Subsequent runs must
match byte-for-byte.
